Mysterious Creatures in Belarusian and British Folklore
Protectors and Helpers

Asilak is a defender of the earth and people. It fights against the powers of darkness and evil.

Dzedka is a mythological creature that gives wealth. It has red fiery eyes and a red beard. Dzedka looks like a poor man with a bag on its shoulder. The Belarusians consider Dzedka to be a keeper of wealth. It appears in the place where the treasure is hidden.

Dzyady are the spirits of the family ancestors. They live together with descend-ants and help them. There is a holiday with the same name in Belarus.
