Mysterious Creatures in Belarusian and British Folklore
Airy Spirits and Creatures

Gartsuky are spirits that cause winds, storms, hurricanes and other destructive natural phenomena.

Povetriki are evil spirits which fly over swamps and in thick forests. They rise from the place together with wind and fly not paying attention to a casual. They can ruin everything on their way.
