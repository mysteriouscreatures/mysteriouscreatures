Airy Spirits and Creatures

Gartsuky are spirits that cause winds, storms, hurricanes and other destructive natu-ral phenomena.

Povetriki are evil spirits which fly over swamps and in thick forests. They rise from the place together with wind and fly not paying attention to a casual. They can ruin every-thing on their way.
